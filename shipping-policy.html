Shipping Policy

Welcome to Brain Infinity! We are committed to delivering your order with speed and accuracy. Below, you will find our shipping policies and procedures.

Order Processing

  • Processing Time: Orders are processed within 1-2 business days (excluding weekends and holidays) after receiving your order confirmation email. You will receive another notification when your order has shipped.
  • Order Changes: If you need to change or cancel your order, please contact us within 24 hours of placing your order. Once your order has been processed, we cannot make any changes.

Shipping Rates and Estimates

  • Shipping Charges: Shipping charges for your order will be calculated and displayed at checkout.
  • Free Shipping: We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $50 within the continental United States.
  • Shipping Methods: We use reliable carriers such as USPS, UPS, and FedEx to ensure timely delivery.
Shipping Method Estimated Delivery Time Cost
Standard 5-7 business days $5.00
Expedited 2-3 business days $15.00
Overnight 1 business day $25.00

International Shipping

  • Availability: We currently ship to the following countries: Canada, United Kingdom, Australia, and Germany.
  • Shipping Rates: International shipping rates will be calculated and displayed at checkout.
  • Duties and Taxes: International orders may be subject to import duties and taxes (including VAT), which are incurred once a shipment reaches your destination country. Brain Infinity is not responsible for these charges if they are applied and are your responsibility as the customer.

Order Tracking

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking number. You can track your order by visiting the carrier's website and entering your tracking number.

Delayed or Lost Packages

  • Delays: Occasionally, there may be delays due to factors beyond our control (e.g., weather, carrier delays). We will do our best to keep you informed if there are any issues with your shipment.
  • Lost Packages: If your order is significantly delayed or lost, please contact us at support@braininfinity.com, and we will work with the carrier to resolve the issue.

Returns and Exchanges

For information about returns and exchanges, please visit our Return Policy page.
